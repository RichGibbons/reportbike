Terms of use

This signature network access agreement (‘agreement’) outlines the terms of use for the sign your mortgage deed service (‘service’).

This service can only be used to view and sign a mortgage deed using an electronic signature supplied by the Chief Land Registrar (‘Land Registry’).

To use this service must:

  • be at least 18 years old
  • have effective security and virus protection on the computer or device you're using
  • keep your login and security details safe and not share them with anyone

Call us on 0300 006 0411 if you think someone has access to your login or security details

We reserve the right to end this agreement and remove your access to this service at any time.

This agreement will end automatically if you die or are made bankrupt.

Your liability

If you break this agreement, you’ll have to compensate HM Land Registry and third parties for any loss or damage they suffer as a result.

If HM Land Registry has caused any of the loss or damage, the compensation you’ll have to pay will be reduced.

HM Land Registry’s liability

Where it’s at fault, HM Land Registry’s standard legal requirement to pay compensation for losses or damages does not change.

There’s no limit on the compensation it has to pay for:

  • death or personal injury resulting from its negligence
  • acts or omissions it makes in bad faith

Additionally, HM Land Registry will compensate you for costs and expenses you incur as a direct result of it acting wrongly under the law, provided these total more than £120 but less than £250.

It does not have to pay compensation for losses or damages caused by:

  • any action it takes as the registrar, by law or which it is entitled to make according to this agreement
  • you breaking the terms of this agreement or the law, unless that happened because HM Land Registry broke this agreement or failed in its duties
  • any damage caused to your computer or device as a result of you failing to provide up-to-date antivirus measures software
  • any damage caused by you or anyone else deliberately altering the service or introducing a virus, worm or other malignant software
  • inaccurate information supplied by the ‘sign your digital mortgage’ service, except where compensation is required by law.

HM Land Registry’s employees and agents are not personally liable for any loss or damage you or anyone else suffers, unless they have acted in bad faith.

Changes to these terms of use

We may, from time-to-time, change the terms of use for this service. This will not be while you’re reading or signing your deed.
