What happens next

Now you have signed your mortgage deed. Next steps:

  • if there are any other borrowers, Samual Montagu and Co. have contacted them with details of how to sign
  • Samual Montagu and Co. will complete their paperwork and send it to HM Land Registry
  • HM Land Registry will then update their records

The time it takes will vary, depending on your situation.

Questions?

If you have any questions about your mortgage deed or the next steps, contact Samual Montagu and Co.
